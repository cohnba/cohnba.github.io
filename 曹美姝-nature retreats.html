NATURAL RETREATS

NATURAL RETREATS GLOBAL PLATFORM


From the star-filled skies of Montana, to the rugged Scottish isles, Natural Retreats connects guests to the great outdoors through expert local insight and bespoke service. As the brand quickly establishes itself as a leader in luxury vacation home and lodge rentals, its digital presence has been a top priority and a critical tool in connecting guests with personalized escapes to some of the world's most stunning natural settings.

In order to amplify the brand's unique value and scale to meet growing demand, DOMANI led the strategy, design, development, and performance marketing efforts for Natural Retreats’ global brand platform.

From browsing featured retreats, to planning and booking an incredible outdoor trek, the digital platform is designed to provide a seamless, intuitive experience across all devices. With helpful tips and recommendations, stunning visuals, ratings and reviews, local area interactive maps, and suggested itineraries, guests are empowered to explore, dream, and discover the ultimate escape.

In the first six months since launch, the new Natural Retreats platform has helped register a nearly 400% improvement in online conversion. DOMANI continues to be a go-to partner for platform enhancements, CRM, and conversion optimization.

- Integrated destination content from concierge teams highlights the local experience
